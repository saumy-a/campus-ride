The Campus Ride Story

It all started with a simple idea: make campus travel easy, affordable, and fun for everyone. As students ourselves, we knew the daily struggle — rushing between lectures, chasing buses, and wishing for a faster, greener solution.

In 2023, Campus Ride was born with a mission to change the way we move. We introduced smart bikes, e-bikes, and e-scooters designed to connect every corner of the campus efficiently and sustainably.
